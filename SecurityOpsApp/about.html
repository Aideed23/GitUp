About Yellow Star Express Taxi

Yellow Star Express Taxi was built around one core belief: every rider deserves safe, punctual, respectful transportation they can trust.

Mission

Deliver transportation that protects access to work, family, and healthcare through reliable service and high operating standards.

Vision

Evolve from a trusted taxi provider into a leading NEMT partner known for quality, coordination, and rider dignity.

Core Values

  • Reliability and accountability
  • Safety and professional conduct
  • Empathy for patient and caregiver needs
  • Continuous process improvement

Serving communities with commitment and professionalism.
